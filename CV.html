Profile

I am a Principal Measurement Technician with a passion for 3D laser scanning, scan data processing, programming, and 3D CAD modeling in the petrochemical industry. With sixteen years of experience, I've honed my skills in scanning and land surveying techniques and equipment to ensure precise scan model accuracy and proper alignment with client-specified plant coordinates. I take pride in my attention to detail and technical expertise, transforming complex data into valuable insights.

Beyond the technical work, I manage various aspects of 3D laser scanning projects, from scoping jobs and preparing quotations to mentoring and training junior scan technicians. I also oversee the calibration and maintenance of our scanning and land surveying equipment, ensuring optimal performance. A significant part of my role involves processing large volumes of scan data using multiple software platforms to create accurate models and data formats tailored to each client's needs. Additionally, I am passionate about programming and problem-solving, always seeking ways to streamline workflows and enhance productivity through automation and efficient coding solutions.

Outside of work, I'm a dedicated husband and father, and my family is my greatest inspiration. I cherish spending quality time with them and always strive to be a positive role model. I also prioritize fitness, as my morning gym sessions keep me motivated and energized for the day ahead.
