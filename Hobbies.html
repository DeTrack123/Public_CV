About myself

Hi, I'm Christo! I am a Principal Measurement Technician who loves 3D laser scanning, scan data, programming, and 3D CAD modeling and The best part? It’s both my passion and my profession.

I enjoy programming and problem-solving, always looking for ways to make work faster and easier through automation. I have built various Excel VBA programs, including a budget tracker, a checklist system for the lab, and a timesheet management tool.

Outside of work, I am a husband and father, and my family is the most important part of my life. I love spending time with them and always try to be a good example. I also enjoy morning gym sessions, which keep me active and motivated for the day.

I have a passion for woodworking. Additionally, I own a 3D printer, which I use for various creative and practical projects.

My Hobbies/Work

My hobbies Description
3D Laser Scanning Capturing precise measurements and analyzing scans
  • Using a 3D laser scanner to capture precise measurements of designated areas.
  • To combine the scans into a 3D point cloud, creative a point cloud in huge projects.
  • CAD models and conducted detailed scans analysis, evaluating parameters such as out-of-roundness and nozzle positioning. Also generated meshes for FEA purposes.
  • Creating 3D models using scan data in AutoCAD, CADWorx, and SpaceClaim for piping, mechanical, and FEA engineering.
Programming Developing automation tools and custom scripts
  • Using VBA in excel to create a budget tracker, a timesheet management tool, and a checklist system for the lab.
  • Additionally, I have explored Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to expand my programming skills.
  • I am thoroughly enjoying the HyperionDev program, which has been instrumental in my programming journey.
